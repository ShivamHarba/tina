FOOTBALL RULES

FOOTBALL
Players: Two teams of 11 players try to score a goal by using any part of their body except their hands and arms
Goalkeeper: The only player who can touch or hold the ball with their hands is the goalkeeper, and they can only do so within their penalty area.
Offside: The offside rule prevents players from easily kicking the ball into the goal by having a teammate lob it across the pitch
