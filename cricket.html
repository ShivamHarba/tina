KNOW about cricket

  1. Protective equipment The wicket-keeper can wear safety equipment like a helmet, shin guards, and webbed mitts. Other players can wear a helmet and shin guards if they are close to the batsmen.
  2. Cricket uniforms Cricketers traditionally wear all-white kit, but in limited overs cricket, they wear club or team colors.
  3. Bowling The bowler from the fielding team bowls the ball toward the striker's wicket.
