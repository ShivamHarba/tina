KNOW ABOUT ICE HOCKY

  1. Players and teams A hockey match is played between two teams, each with up to 11 players on the field at once.
  2. Players' equipment Players must use a stick with a rounded head to play the ball. Goalkeepers can use their feet, hands, and other parts of their body with their stick.
  3. Goalkeepers Goalkeepers must wear protective headgear at all times, except when taking a penalty stroke. They are only permitted to play the ball with their stick when the ball is outside the circle they are defending.
