RULES OF WFC

  • Licenses: All participants, including fencers, coaches, and referees, must have an EFC license. National federations are responsible for entering participants.
  • Referee quotas: The number of referees required depends on the number of fencers competing. For example, 1–3 fencers require no referee, while 10–15 fencers require 2 referees.
  • Competition formats: Competitions often include one or two rounds of pools followed by a direct elimination table.
